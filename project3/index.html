Turkish Baklava

Baklava is a layered pastry dessert made of filo pastry sheets, filled with chopped nuts, and sweetened with syrup or honey. It was one of the most popular sweet pastries in the Ottoman Empire.

Author: Hakan ADALI

Servings: 4-6 people

Servings: 4-6 people

Servings: 4-6 people

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups of water
  • 4 cups of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 3 cups of flour
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/4 cup of oil
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 200 g melted butter
  • 2 cups of crushed pistachios
  • Cornstarch

Instructions:

  1. For Syrup you need:
    4 cups of Water, 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract, 4 cups of sugar and wait the water to boil.
  2. For dough you need :
    3 cups of flour, 1 egg, 2 teaspoon of baking powder, 1/4 cup of oil, 1 cup of milk and mix together to make the dough.
  3. Divide the dough into 14 pieces where the total makes 28 pieces. 14 for part 1 and 14 for part 2.
  4. For filling you need :
    200g Melted butter, cornstarch, pistachio. Put as much pistachio as you can to taste great.
  5. Bake at 150*c for 45-50 min
  6. When the dough is bake add syrup, it should be cold and the dough should be hot.
  7. Let Baklava rest for 6 hours for the best result.
